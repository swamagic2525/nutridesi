Why this exists

I coached people for years and watched nearly every one of them quit a tracking app. Not because the numbers were wrong — because opening an app at 9pm after dinner is friction nobody sustains.

So I built this where you already are. It's honest about what it is: home-cooked Indian food logs at roughly ±15–20%, because database values are averages, not your kitchen. That's still enough to change what you eat. Showing up beats precision.
